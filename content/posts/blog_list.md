### Hacking
- [Eric S. Raymond's Home Page](http://www.catb.org/~esr/) 

  My favorites are [*How To Become A Hacker*](http://www.catb.org/~esr/faqs/hacker-howto.html), [*How To Learn Hacking*](http://www.catb.org/~esr/faqs/hacking-howto.html#idm45418026902272) and [*The Loginataka*](http://catb.org/~esr/faqs/loginataka.html).

- [Bob Nystrom](https://journal.stuffwithstuff.com/)

  I simply love every single essay he writes.

- [Peter Norvig](https://norvig.com/)

  My favorite is of course the legendary [*Teach Yourself Programming in Ten Years*](https://norvig.com/21-days.html).

- [Paul Graham](https://paulgraham.com/index.html)

- [Ben Hoyt's projects](https://benhoyt.com/projects/)
